The Story of Taste

In the vibrant streets of Bhavnagar, Bunty Kaka started with a simple dream: to create the perfect Bateta Bhungla. With a secret blend of spices and a passion for quality, he turned a small cart into a culinary landmark.

Today, "Bunty Kaka Bhavnagari" is not just a shop; it's an emotion. From the "Kids Level" for the little ones to the legendary "Rocket Spicy" for the brave, every plate serves a memory.
