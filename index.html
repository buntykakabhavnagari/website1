Our Story

From a Dream
to Bhavnagar's Heart

It all began with a humble cart, a bag of potatoes, and an unshakeable love for the streets of Bhavnagar. Bunty Kaka grew up watching his mother fry the crispiest Bateta Bhungla in their tiny kitchen — the aroma floating out into warm Gujarat evenings.

After years in the city, he returned to his roots with one simple mission: to share that same honest, authentic, street-side taste with every person who crossed his path. He started small — a few plates, a handful of neighbours, and a recipe perfected over two generations.

Word spread faster than the spice levels. Today, Bunty Kaka Bhavnagari is more than a food stall — it's a gathering place, a memory, a feeling.

"Khana sirf pet bhar ne ke liye nahi hota — dil bhi bharna chahiye."
— Bunty Kaka, Founder
